About "Website Name"

"INSERT NAME HERE" is brought to you by the Open Source Robotics Foundation (OSRF). "INSERT NAME HERE" is a place for you to share your 3D models for use in simulation environments like Gazebo. Share anything from a complex, high degree of freedom robot, to a model of a building, a tool, or a tree. By providing a database for freely-available 3D models, we hope to advance the state of robotic simulation by decreasing the barrier to entry for simulating a robot in a "realistic" simulation environment.

The OSRF Mission

"To support the development, distribution, and adoption of open source software for use in robotics
research, education, and product development."
